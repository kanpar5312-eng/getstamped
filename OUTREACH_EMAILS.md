## Notes on use

- Send in small batches (10-15 at a time), not a blast — you'll want to
  personalize [Name] and add one specific line per recipient (their school,
  their consultancy, their country) or these read as templates, which kills
  response rate.
- Track opens/replies in a simple sheet: name, type (DSO/consultant/applicant),
  date sent, response y/n, feedback theme. You'll want this for the "outcome
  feedback loop" gap (#9 on your list) later anyway.
- Do not attach pricing or upgrade language anywhere in the first email. If
  they ask what the paid tiers include, answer directly, but don't lead with it.
